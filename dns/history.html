Founders & Pioneers

Founding Administrators

The visionaries who created and established the Cyber Range from the ground up.

Lucas Kossack

Founder & Creator

Class of 2018

Lucas was the initial creator of the Cyber Range back in 2015. What started as an independent side project quickly blossomed into a service available for all students on campus and in the surrounding community. The Cyber Range would not have been nearly as far developed without his motivation and dedication to the university.

Visionary Founder Infrastructure Design

Jonathan Fulling

Co-Creator

Class of 2018

Jonathan is the co-creator of the Cyber Range who helped kickstart the very first deployment and expanded the intended capabilities of the original idea. Together, Jonathan and Lucas were able to create a successful and isolated environment used by the school's Collegiate Cyber Defense Team for practice.

Deployment System Architecture CCDC Support

Mackenzie Bonneville

Administrator

Class of 2019

Mackenzie was great friends with both Lucas and Jonathan who developed the Cyber Range. After they graduated, she assumed the administrative role to keep developing the laboratory. Working with the university, the Cyber Range was highly publicized as a key point of the program. She aided in a large expansion of the project that set up the Cyber Range to be in its current state today.

Administration University Relations Program Expansion

Our Mission & Values

The principles that guide our work and commitment to cybersecurity education.

Hands-On Learning

We believe in learning by doing. Our isolated environment allows students to safely practice real-world security scenarios.

Student Leadership

From its inception, the Cyber Range has been built and maintained by students, fostering leadership and real-world experience.

Industry Readiness

Our goal is to prepare students for the challenges they'll face in the cybersecurity industry through realistic training.
